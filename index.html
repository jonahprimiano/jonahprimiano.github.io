Jonah Primiano

Jonah Primiano is a Los Angeles based artist working with graphite and animated filmmaking. His films have been screened at festivals such as the Slamdance Film Festival, London International Animation Festival, Animateka Festival of Animated Film, and Rockaway Film Festival. In 2019, he launched the animation magazine Mostly Moving. He has programmed curated screenings for the Ottawa International Animation Festival, Rockaway Film Festival, Anibar International Animation Festival, and GIRAF Festival of Independent Animation. In 2021, he graduated with an MFA in Experimental Animation from CalArts and was awarded a Post-Graduate Teaching Fellowship. He currently works for CalArts as a Special Faculty in Experimental Animation and the Lead Animation Technical Specialist for the School of Film/Video.
